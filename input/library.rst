Formulas
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

* Kv(v) = RPM
* max kw - (.30) = max continuous power
* Motor efficiency: Used power - heat. Heat is P=i squared * amps (resistance squared times amps)
