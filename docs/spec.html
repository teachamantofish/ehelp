Specing out a System (Sizing)¶

Navigating to final choices and a working system is difficult and time consuming. Until the eHelp harness idea is more widespread, advice will be scattered and design principles scattered across various Facebook pages and forums with pioneers helping where they can. With luck, the concepts here will gain enough traction that the hang gliding community can emulate what’s happening with OpenPPG

Until then, moving forward is a bit of work.
